Privacy Policy

Your Data & Privacy

Our Commitment

At COSMIC HIMALAYA, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or book an adventure with us.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us to facilitate your adventure experience:

  • Personal Details: Name, age, gender, and contact information.
  • Safety Data: Medical history, fitness certificates, and emergency contact details.
  • Booking Info: Payment details and travel preferences.

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely to:

  • Process your bookings and obtain trekking permits.
  • Ensure your health and safety during high-altitude activities.
  • Communicate essential trip details and weather updates.
  • Improve our services and customer support.

Data Protection

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide our services (e.g., local permits or insurance) or comply with legal obligations. Sensitive medical data is handled with strict confidentiality.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. For any privacy-related queries, please contact us at info@cosmichimalaya.com.
